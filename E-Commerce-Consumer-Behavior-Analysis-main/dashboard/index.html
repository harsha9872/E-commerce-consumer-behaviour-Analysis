💡 Key Insights & Recommendations

1

Purchase Intent is Multi-dimensional

Driven by research time, brand loyalty, and discount sensitivity — not just demographics.

🎯 Personalization engines should weight behavioral signals over demographic ones
2

Loyalty ≠ Higher Spending

Program members don't necessarily spend more — the program may attract deal-seekers.

💡 Redesign loyalty tiers to reward high-spend behavior
3

Social Media Influence Varies

Younger customers (18-35) are significantly more influenced by social media.

📱 Allocate social media ad budget to 18-35 demographic
4

Returns Correlate with Impulse

Impulsive buyers have higher return rates across all categories.

🔄 Implement "cooling off" features (wishlists, save-for-later)
5

Research = Satisfaction

Product research time correlates with higher customer satisfaction scores.

📝 Invest in detailed product pages, comparison tools, reviews
6

Middle-Income = Most Discount Sensitive

Not low-income as expected — middle-income customers respond most to discounts.

🏷️ Target discount campaigns at middle-income segments
7

8 Distinct Customer Segments

K-Means clustering reveals distinct behavioral profiles enabling targeted marketing.

🎨 Deploy segment-specific campaigns based on cluster profiles

🚀 Strategic Recommendations

📣 Marketing Optimization

  • Deploy segment-specific campaigns based on K-Means clusters
  • Increase social media budget for 18-35 audience
  • Use personalized discount strategies for middle-income
  • Leverage product ratings prominently

🔄 Customer Retention

  • Redesign loyalty program with tiered spend-based rewards
  • Post-purchase engagement for impulsive buyers
  • Re-engagement campaigns for declining frequency
  • Exclusive early access for high brand-loyalty customers

💰 Revenue Growth

  • Cross-sell based on category purchase patterns
  • Optimize pricing for high-performing categories
  • Reduce friction in low-satisfaction channels
  • Invest in mobile shopping experience

📊 Data-Driven Framework

  • Use Purchase Intent model for homepage personalization
  • Deploy Satisfaction model to identify at-risk customers
  • Use Loyalty Prediction for member conversion targeting
  • Continuously update segments as behavior evolves
